About Us

MeowMatcha, where the serene charm of traditional Japanese tea culture meets the playful purr of feline companionship. Nestled in the heart of Bali, our café is a haven for matcha enthusiasts and cat lovers alike. Step into our cozy space, adorned with minimalist décor accented by whimsical cat-themed artwork. The gentle aroma of freshly brewed matcha fills the air, inviting you to unwind and savor the moment

At MeowMatcha, we take pride in serving the finest quality matcha sourced from Japan's lush tea fields. From classic matcha lattes to indulgent matcha-infused desserts, our menu offers a delightful array of options to satisfy your cravings. Whether you prefer your matcha bold and earthy or subtly sweetened, our skilled baristas are dedicated to crafting the perfect cup to suit your taste.
